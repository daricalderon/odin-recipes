Easy Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this delicious lasagna recipe:

  • Beef: This lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey if you opt for a lighter option.
  • Spaghetti sauce: You can use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce.
  • Cheeses: For this recipe you'll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan
  • Eggs: They will help you bind the cheese mixture together. Plus they lend moisture and richness.
  • Seasonings: Season the easy lasagna with dried parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Of course, some lasagna noodles!
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.

Steps to Make Delicious Lasagna

Here's the step-by-step recipe

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cotagge cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
